Title of research study: Advancing Artificial Intelligence for the Naval Domain

This consent form asks you to take part in a research study. The study is being conducted by Dr. Elizabeth Marsh, PhD., professor at the Department of Psychology and Neuroscience, Duke University.

Key Information

Purpose: To better understand how people learn with the goal of informing learning in naval contexts What you will be asked to do: This research study consists of one session. You will be asked to learn instances of a category such as rocks. Then you will see new exemplars (new rocks) and be asked to categorize them. You will also be asked to provide some demographic information. The total time for participating in this research study is expected to take between 25-30min. At the end of the study you will be compensated $4. Participation in this study is voluntary. You will be compensated after the end of the session only if you complete the study. To stop, click on the “Return HIT” button, or close your browser window.

How your confidentiality will be maintained: If you choose to participate, your name will not be linked to your responses at any time, and there is no link between this consent form and your responses. However, we will collect your Amazon Mechanical Turk worker identification number for payment purposes and record keeping. We will collect your worker ID on the next page before you begin the study. All data will be securely saved at all times. All data we keep will be de-identified, indefinitely. Data with direct identifiers removed may be used for future research or shared with other researchers.

Benefits and Risks: This research will not benefit you personally. The researcher will be happy to discuss the details of the study with you in detail at its completion. We know of no risks to you for participating in this study.

If you have any questions about this research, please ask the experimenter who set up this “HIT”. You may also contact the Dr. Elizabeth Marsh (marshlab@duke.edu) if you have any questions about the study. You may contact the Duke University Campus Institutional Review Board at (919)-684-3030 or at campusirb@duke.edu about your rights as a research participant.

Clicking Accept: By clicking on the “Accept HIT” button, you indicate that you are 18 years of age or older, that you voluntarily agree to participate in this study and that you understand the information in this consent form. You have not waived any legal rights you otherwise would have as a participant in a research study.
